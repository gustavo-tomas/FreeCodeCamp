Alan Mathison Turing

Alan Turing is know for his work in some fields of computer science. Here's a timeline of his life:

  • 1912 - Born in Maida Vale, London
  • 1934 - Graduated from King's College in mathematics
  • 1936 - Turing publishes his paper "On Computable Numbers, with an Application to the Entscheidungsproblem".
  • 1938 - In June, he obtained his PhD from the Department of Mathematics at Princeton
  • 1939 - Helps the British codebreaking organization to develop a solution to Enigma machine's during the Second World War
  • 1952 - Turing is convicted of indecency for having a relationship with another man
  • 1954 - Dies at age 41 from cyanide poisoning
  • 2009 - A petition urging for a government apology regarding Turing's conviction is started. In the same year, the apology is acknowledged
